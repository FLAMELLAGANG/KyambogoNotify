COMMON QUERIES

Frequently asked questions

How do I access the Student Portal notifications?

add

Simply login using your institutional email and navigate to the Notify dashboard to see all personalized updates.

Can I customize my notification preferences?

add

Yes! Once signed in, visit your Workspace to manage which categories you receive alerts for — Examinations, Finance, Events and more.

How often are the notices updated?

add

Notices are published in real time as they are approved by the moderation team. Urgent notices are pushed immediately with SMS and push notification alerts.

Is there a mobile app available?

add

A progressive web app version is accessible on all mobile browsers. Simply visit notify.kyu.ac.ug and tap "Add to Home Screen" on your device.
